|Where We Fly

Singapore Airlines, alongside our low-cost subsidiary Scoot, directly serves over 120 destinations across more than 35 countries spanning six continents.
Operating from our world-class hub at Singapore Changi Airport, the airline boasts an exceptionally robust network across the Asia-Pacific region, linking travelers to key hubs in China, India, Australia, and Japan.
We is also famous for operating some of the world's longest commercial flights, connecting Singapore nonstop to North American gateways like New York, Newark, Los Angeles, and San Francisco

Beyond our own fleet, Singapore Airlines massively expands its global reach through its membership in the Star Alliance network.
This alliance, paired with strategic joint ventures and codeshare agreements with major carriers like Lufthansa, All Nippon Airways (ANA), Air New Zealand, and Malaysia Airlines, unlocks seamless connections to thousands of additional cities.
Through these partnerships, passengers can easily book coordinated itineraries to trickier regions—like domestic destinations inside the United States, deep into regional Europe, or across South America and Africa.
